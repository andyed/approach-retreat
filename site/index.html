Every approach-retreat episode becomes a PostHog event with the full 19-field flatten (position, outcome, dwell, retreat distance, velocity, forward/regressive direction, etc.) plus a 10% downsampled cursor trajectory (xytvxvy/1 format).

Four-class taxonomy splits the non-click outcomes into deferred (retreated but re-approached), evaluated_rejected (retreated and never returned), and not_approached (visible but cursor never entered the AOI).

Session summary fires on visibilitychange/pagehide with per-position classification counts and time-to-first-click.
